## Coming from Thailand?

We also publish the [NomadReady Thailand Guide](/thailand). If you've used that guide, you'll feel right at home here. Key differences between Thailand and Bali:

- **Visa:** Bali's E33G ($60K income) is harder to qualify for than Thailand's DTV
- **Transport:** No BTS/MRT — you'll need a motorbike in Bali
- **Payments:** QRIS/GoPay vs PromptPay — different systems, same convenience
- **Safety:** Motorbike risk is significantly higher in Bali
- **Community:** Both have huge nomad scenes, but Bali's is more concentrated (Canggu)
- **Cost:** Similar overall, but Bali offers cheaper luxury villas
