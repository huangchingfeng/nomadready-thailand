# Chapter 4: Coworking Spaces

> **Bali's coworking scene is mature, competitive, and surprisingly affordable.** From bamboo open-air spaces in rice fields to air-conditioned tech hubs with pools — there's a workspace for every style and budget.

---

| **Puco Rooftop** | 200K IDR ($13) | ~2.5M IDR ($158) | 100 Mbps | Rooftop + pool |

### Our Top Picks

**Best All-Round: BWork** (Berawa)
- The most complete package in Canggu. 150-270 Mbps fiber with backup lines. Pool, gym, yoga sessions. Open 24/7 for members. Regular community events and workshops. The monthly price (IDR 2.5-3.2M) is fair for what you get.

**Best for Social Nomads: Tropical Nomad** (Batu Bolong)
- The "living room" of Canggu's nomad scene. Open-concept design encourages interaction. Free trial available — try before you commit. Strong events calendar. Not ideal for deep focus work (noise level is high).

**Best Budget: Nebula** (Canggu)
- At IDR 1.75M/month ($110), this is the cheapest dedicated workspace with reliable 120-180 Mbps internet. Boutique feel, quieter than the big names. Dedicated desk option at IDR 3.8M for those who want a permanent spot.

**Best for Coliving: Tribal** (Canggu)
- Combined coworking + coliving + pool + gym in one membership. If you're new to Bali and want instant community + workspace, this is your one-stop solution. No entrance fee if you just want to try for a few hours with a drink purchase.

> ⚠️ **Dojo Bali** — the legendary Canggu coworking space near Echo Beach — **permanently closed in December 2022.** Many articles and guides still list it. It's gone. Don't go looking for it.

## Ubud

### Quick Comparison

| Space | Day Pass | Monthly | WiFi | Best Feature |
|-------|----------|---------|------|-------------|
| **Outpost Nyuh Kuning** | 250K IDR ($16) | ~3.6M IDR ($230) | 200 Mbps | Best internet in Ubud |
| **Outpost Penestanan** | 230K IDR ($15) | ~3.3M IDR ($210) | 180 Mbps | Pool + quiet |
| **Hubud** | 200K IDR ($13) | 1-4.3M IDR ($60-275) | Fiber | Pioneer, bamboo architecture |
| **Beluna** | 200K IDR ($13) | ~2.8M IDR ($180) | 150 Mbps | Rice field views |
| **Ubud Co-Working** | 200K IDR ($13) | ~3M IDR ($190) | 100+ Mbps | Spacious, quiet |

### Our Top Picks

**Best for Reliability: Outpost Ubud** (Nyuh Kuning + Penestanan)
- Two locations, both excellent. Nyuh Kuning has the fastest internet in Ubud (200 Mbps). Penestanan has a pool and is surrounded by rice fields. Air-conditioned rooms, private call booths, and genuine community events. The gold standard.

**Best for the Experience: Hubud** (Central Ubud)
- Bali's first coworking space, opened 2013. The famous bamboo architecture isn't just for Instagram — it's genuinely beautiful to work in. Flexible memberships from hourly to unlimited. Community-driven events have been running for over a decade. Fiber internet.

**Best for Creatives: Beluna House of Creatives**
- Work surrounded by rice fields in a bamboo structure. 150 Mbps internet. Attracts writers, designers, and artists. Monthly at $180 is a fair price for the environment. Not for people who need corporate-grade facilities.

## Sanur

Limited dedicated coworking, but **Genius Cafe** on the beachfront is the go-to spot — reliable WiFi, good coffee, ocean view. Most long-term Sanur residents work from home with fiber internet.

---

## Free/Cheap Alternatives

Not ready to commit to a membership? These work:

- **Cafe hopping:** Many Bali cafes have 50-100 Mbps WiFi and don't mind laptop workers. Buy a coffee every 2-3 hours.
- **Villa with fiber:** If you're staying 1+ month, negotiate fiber internet in your villa (IDR 500K-1M/month extra)
- **Coliving spaces:** Many include coworking in the rent price
- **Hotel lobbies:** Some upscale hotels tolerate discreet laptop workers in their lobbies/cafes

---

## Tips for Choosing

1. **Try before you buy.** Most spaces offer day passes or free trials. Use them.
2. **Internet is non-negotiable.** Ask for a speed test, not just the advertised speed.
3. **Check the vibe.** Social butterflies thrive at Tropical Nomad; deep workers need Outpost or Nebula.
4. **Location matters.** A cheap space 30 minutes from your villa = you won't go.
5. **24/7 access.** If you work US/EU time zones, make sure your space is open when you need it.
